What does this shipping module do?

This shipping module integrates with Freightquote.com to provide instant quotes on freight shipments at checkout. This will also modify the product editor in your store's administration so that you can specificy shipping details for each product in your store.

How to Use

Step 1 - Enable the Freightquote.com Shipping Module
  1. Log into your store's administration and go to Modules -> Shipping
  2. Click on the "Freightquote.com" module and then "Install" to install the module.
  3. Configure the module using your username and password and cutomize the other settings to best fit your shipping practices.
Step 2 - Configure Your Products

Edit each of your products and configure the options found in the box labeled, "Freightquote.com Shipping Information." Remember, only products with "Enable with Freightquote" checked will be included in shipping quotes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need an account with Freightquote.com to use this service?

Yes, you can sign up for a free account by clicking here.

Q: Why does my shipping quote say that products were excluded?

Products that have not been enabled to use the Freightquote.com shipping method or are missing required information will be excluded from the quote. Check all of your products and ensure that all required fields have been completed.

Q: The module is enabled but does not appear as a shipping option. Why?

If no products in the customer's cart have their shipping options configured, the module will disable itself instead of displaying an error. The best practice is to enable free shipping under Modules -> Order Totals -> Shipping and then change the "Free Shipping" language to something that informs the customer that their shipping costs will be calculated afterwards.
